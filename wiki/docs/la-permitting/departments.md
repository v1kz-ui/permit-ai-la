# LA City Permitting Departments

Fire rebuild permits in Los Angeles require clearances from multiple city departments. PermitAI tracks all 10 departments plus LA County.

## Department Overview

### 1. LADBS -- Department of Building & Safety
**Code:** `ladbs` | **Website:** [ladbs.org](https://www.ladbs.org)

The primary permitting authority. Issues building permits and conducts inspections.

**Clearances:**
- Building Plan Check (always required)
- Structural Review (always required)
- Zoning Clearance (always required)
- Hillside Grading Review (hillside properties)
- Geotechnical Review (geological hazard zones)

---

### 2. DCP -- Department of City Planning
**Code:** `dcp` | **Website:** [planning.lacity.gov](https://planning.lacity.gov)

Handles zoning, land use, and environmental review.

**Clearances:**
- Zoning Conformance Verification (always required)
- Coastal Development Permit (coastal zone)
- Specific Plan Compliance (specific plan areas)
- HPOZ Board Review (Historic Preservation Overlay Zones)
- Historic Preservation Review (historic properties)

---

### 3. BOE -- Bureau of Engineering
**Code:** `boe`

Reviews public works impacts including sewers, streets, and drainage.

**Clearances:**
- Sewer Connection/Capacity Clearance (always required)
- Street/Sidewalk Improvement Requirements
- Haul Route Approval (hillside demolition debris)
- Hillside Drainage Review (hillside properties)
- Flood Zone Review (flood zones)

---

### 4. LAFD -- Los Angeles Fire Department
**Code:** `lafd` | **Website:** [lafd.org](https://www.lafd.org)

Reviews fire and life safety compliance.

**Clearances:**
- Fire/Life Safety Plan Review (always required)
- Brush Clearance Verification (Very High Fire Severity Zones)
- Fire Flow/Hydrant Adequacy (Very High Fire Severity Zones)
- Fire Sprinkler Plan Review

---

### 5. LADWP -- Department of Water & Power
**Code:** `ladwp` | **Website:** [ladwp.com](https://www.ladwp.com)

Manages water and electrical service connections.

**Clearances:**
- Water Service Connection Clearance (always required)
- Electrical Service Connection Clearance (always required)
- Meter Reset/Reconnection

---

### 6. LASAN -- LA Sanitation & Environment
**Code:** `lasan`

Reviews sewer connections and environmental compliance.

**Clearances:**
- LID (Low Impact Development) Stormwater Compliance (always required)
- Green Streets Requirement
- Construction Waste Recycling Plan

---

### 7. LAHD -- LA Housing Department
**Code:** `lahd`

Handles multi-family housing compliance.

**Clearances:**
- Rent Stabilization Ordinance Clearance (multi-unit properties)
- Housing Compliance Review
- Tenant Relocation Assistance (if applicable)

---

### 8. DOT -- Department of Transportation
**Code:** `dot`

Reviews transportation and access impacts.

**Clearances:**
- Driveway/Curb Cut Access Review
- Traffic Impact Analysis (major projects)

---

### 9. Cultural Affairs -- Department of Cultural Affairs
**Code:** `cultural_affairs`

Reviews cultural and archaeological resources.

**Clearances:**
- Cultural/Archaeological Resource Review (historic properties)
- Public Art Requirement Compliance (projects over $500K)

---

### 10. Urban Forestry -- Urban Forestry Division (Public Works)
**Code:** `urban_forestry`

Manages protected trees on public and private property.

**Clearances:**
- Protected Tree Removal/Relocation Permit
- Tree Replacement Plan Compliance

---

### LA County
**Code:** `la_county`

For projects in unincorporated areas or requiring county-level review.

**Clearances:**
- Geotechnical/Soils Report Review (geological hazard areas)
- Flood Zone Compliance Review (flood zones)
